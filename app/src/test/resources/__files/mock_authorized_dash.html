Welcome to the DCPS MyGrade Portal
Accessing the DCPS MyGrade Portal is a great way to help ensure your student’s academic success. This wonderful tool allows parents to see their student’s homework, school calendar, teacher websites, grades, and attendance all at your fingertips. Also, be sure to check out all of the digital resources to help maximize their learning.  Click on this link to learn more about the dcps.mygradeportal.com

Parent Academy
The Parent Academy is a family resource designed for parents, caregivers, and community members to promote involvement and enhance student achievement. classes, activities, and resources. The Parent Academy's focus is in three strands: Student Achievement, Parenting & Advocacy, and Personal & Individual Growth. Click on the link to learn more Parent Academy.
